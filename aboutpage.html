About Us

Artsy Frames Photography is a modern photography studio located in Nairobi. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality service for our clients and we strive to make their special moments timeless. We offer a wide range of services from portraits, family, weddings and more.

Artsy Frames Photography is dedicated to providing our clients with the best quality photos and frames for their memories. It all started in 2019. I (Godfrey) had been shooting professionally for about 2 years when I met Kelvin, we discovered that we shared much of the same artistic vision and enjoyed collaborating on projects together. Over time, we would meet up once or twice per month and shoot together, never charging clients or art directors. We set out to create a company in which we could have complete creative control: one where our vision would be respected and upheld. And so Artsy Frames photography was born - as a studio focusing largely on lifestyle shoots.
